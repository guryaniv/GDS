Submissions are evaluated on the total amount of weight you fit into Santa's
1000 bags. The rules that govern gift packing are as follows: Overfilling a
bag above the 50lb limit will cause the entire bag to count for nothing,
without warning! No gift may be used more than once. Every bag must have 3 or
more gifts. Submission File For each bag, you must provide a space-delimited
list of GiftIds. Each line represents one bag. The file should contain a
header and have the following format: Gifts horse_0 book_345 gloves_48
doll_873 train_714 coal23 bike_85 etc.
