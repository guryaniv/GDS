Goal The goal of this competition is to predict the category of a product
based on its image(s). Note that a product can have one or several images
associated. For every product _id in the test set, you should predict the
correct category_id. Metric This competition is evaluated on the
categorization accuracy of your predictions (the percentage of products you
get correct). Submission File For each _id in the test set, you must predict a
category_id. The file should contain a header and have the following format:
_id,category_id 2,1000000055 5,1000016018 6,1000016055 etc.
